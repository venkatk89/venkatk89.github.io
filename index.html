Bio

Firstly, Hello!

Solving problems has always excited me. My propensity to analyze complex problems uprooted me from my small hometown and placed me at IIT Madras to pursue an undergraduate degree in Engineering Physics.

When tasked to find the value of Plank’s constant in a photoelectric experimental setup, I bumped into a paradigm of problem-solving called data science. I delved deeper and pursued it as a career after graduation. As a data science consultant, I gained expertise in employing machine learning algorithms to develop innovative solutions in diverse fields of FinTech, e-commerce, and marketing.

After collaborating with companies of varying profiles ranging from fortune 500s to small businesses, I ascertained that academia would be the best fit for me to realize the positive impact I want to create in this world. I started my research career as a Junior Research Fellow at The Centre for Integrative Biology and Systems Medicine, IIT Madras.
