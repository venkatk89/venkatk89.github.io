Bio

Venkatesh is an autodidactic data scientist and a musician who is not comfortable with addressing himself in the third person.

Firstly, Hello!

Solving problems have always excited me. My propensity to solve complex problems uprooted me from my hometown and planted me in the Indian Institute of Technology Madras to pursue an undergraduate degree in Engineering Physics.

Amazing things start from simple means. When tasked to calculate the value of Plank's constant from a photoelectric effect experimental setup, I first came upon the term linear regression. I was intrigued and delved deeper into the field of statistical computing. The interest was unabating, and I started to pursue data science as a career.

As a data scientist, I've collaborated with companies of varying profiles, ranging from fortune 500's to small businesses. I've successfully applied my skills to solve some central problems in the areas of e-commerce, fintech, logistics, and marketing to gain domain expertise.

Asking questions is one of the best ways to grow. It was a question of the impact that helped me realize that some problems are more crucial. This realization encouraged me to embark on a journey:
To solve problems whose solutions have a positive impact on this world.

I started to learn and dig deeper into bioinformatics. I aspire to learn state-of-the-art and be on the cutting-edge of this field.
