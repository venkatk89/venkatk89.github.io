About Me

I play the Harmonium, Melodica, Ukulele, and the Kazoo. I am very much excited to work with MPE-supported electronic instruments. I am an avid listener of Tamil Filmi music and have recently developed a taste in Western and Indian classical music.

I love to improvise on the works of great composers while desperately trying to maintain the intricacies. If you are interested in experiencing the musical mistakes that I make, follow this link.
